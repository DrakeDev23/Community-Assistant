What is Barangay Service?

Barangay services are community-based assistance programs provided by local government units to address the needs, concerns, and safety of residents.

These services include issuing documents, responding to emergencies, handling complaints, and ensuring peace and order within the community.

Submit Request

Fill out the service request form with accurate details about your concern.

Review Process

Barangay officials review your request to determine the appropriate action.

Processing

The request is processed and coordinated with the proper Barangay department or personnel.

Completion

Once completed, you will be notified about the status or resolution of your request.
